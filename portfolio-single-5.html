Exploit The Hacker

Project Details

  • Category : Arcade Game
  • Client : For fun
  • My role : Game design, Producer & UX design

Exploit The Hacker is an arcade game developed with the purpose of entertaining and give purpose to an unused location of my university. This project was chosen by the Interactive design career director to be presented in the biggest event of videogames in Colombia, called SOFA (link at the end).

The context of the arcade is hacking. We approach it as a kind of PONG game with new mechanics, a narrative that immerses more the player and innovative way of moving your character : the rotation of your chair. The narrative is about two hackers trying to hack each otherâ€™s servers, where you have to shoot exploits as attacks and deploy patchers as defenses (real life hacking concepts).
