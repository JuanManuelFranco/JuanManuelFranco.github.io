Proteq

UX/UIBrandingApp Design

The problem:
Bogota's horrific traffic problems grow every day. The city has more cars than it can handle. Finding a parking space nowadays takes a lot of time and is extremely expensive. A driver could spend 25 minutes finding a spot. This small amount of time increases traffic, makes people park in illegals places and boosts the stress of drivers.

The solution:
Proteq is a digital platform for easy and cheap parking. The app allows drivers to find a spot anytime with just a click and it lets people get extra income for renting their parking spaces whenever they want.

Exploit The Hacker

Arcade GameUX/UIBranding

Exploit The Hacker is an arcade game developed with the purpose of entertaining and give purpose to an unused location of my university. This project was chosen by the Interactive design career director to be presented in the biggest event of videogames in Colombia, called SOFA (link at the end).

The context of the arcade is hacking. We approach it as a kind of PONG game with new mechanics, a narrative that immerses more the player and innovative way of moving your character : the rotation of your chair. The narrative is about two hackers trying to hack each other’s servers, where you have to shoot exploits as attacks and deploy patchers as defenses (real life hacking concepts).
