Anarco Pink

Project Details

  • Category : Mobile Game
  • Client : For fun
  • My role : Unity developer, Producer & UX design

Anarko Pink is transmedia project composed by 3 products. A videogame, an augmented reality fanzine and a web serie. I was in charge of the game, which is a narrative-Platform-Mobile game.

The big picture of the products is the evolution of punk as a movement in Bogotá, Colombia. We approach it in 3 different moments of history: The beginning of punk, the moment when people idealize punk as a bad thing and lastly the renaissance of punk in the future.​​​​
