Meatspace - Facial recognition advertisement

Project Details

  • Category : Web app design

  • For : Meatspace as freelancer.

  • Timeline : Jun 2020 - Nov 2020

  • My role : UX/UI Designer

  • Software : XD adobe, Photoshop

Description:

Meatspace is a startup based in Madrid, Spain. The company uses AI software to power facial recognition advertisements for the outdoors.

David, the founder, came to Aleatorio Estudio looking for a UI designer for meatspace's first MVP of their main website and the internal dashboard of the service.

I was the designer in charge of this challenge. Sadly, I can't show any of the UX research lent to me during this project.
